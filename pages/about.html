About Our Company

"The story of the Louis Vuitton brand begins when, in the year 1837, a 16 years old boy named Louis Vuitton took a walk to Paris and learned the profession of box and packaging expert Monsieur Marshall. He trained in the profession for 17 years until he was finally able to set up his workshop."

"Louis Vuitton was founded in 1947 by Louis Vuitton in France. The company manufactures many luxury goods including luggage, leather goods, shoes, watches, jewelry and sunglasses, and sunglasses, and most of them use their famous LV logo to design. Louis Vuitton is one of the largest international fashion houses in the world offering its products not only in luxury stores but also through its online store."
